Sign Up

Who's In
The live head count. A real-time player list for every day so you can stop playing detective in the group text. No more "Wait, I thought you were playing."

Randos
The Great Equalizer. Automatically builds foursomes as players sign up and reshuffles the deck if someone joins or bails. No politics, just the draw—and of course, it's just a suggestion.

Fore!cast
Know before you go. High temps, conditions, and rain percentages for every active day are baked right into the signup page. A 16-day range covers both weekends.

Pics
The digital clubhouse wall. Post the birdies, the celebrations, and the drive that finally went straight. These photos live forever—they never wipe week-to-week.
🐪
The Bunker
The shame cave. A second photo wall with a custom name for your group. For the fat wedges, the 4-putts, and the shots that should never be spoken of again.
🏆
Hole in One
The Immortal Realm. A password-protected wall for those who've achieved the impossible. If it's not posted here, it didn't happen. This wall means something.

The Playa Club
The leaderboard for the grinders. All-time round tracker with Gold, Silver, and Bronze rankings. Only completed rounds count—sign up and bail, and you might not be invited back.
⚙️
The Commissioner
Keep the line moving. The app automatically resets the weekend and keeps your future sign-ups organized. Use the Friday and Monday toggles to open up the course for long weekends or retirement rounds.
