A Full 18 of Features

Everything your group needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

No app download. No accounts. No sheet-tee setup. Just a link your group bookmarks and uses every weekend.

📅
Sign Up
Two weekends always visible. Sign up for this weekend or lock in next in advance. Friday and Monday toggles. Dates auto-update every week.
👥
Who's In
Live count and full player list for every day. Updates in real time. No more "wait, I thought you were playing."
🎲
Randos — The Fairway Shuffle
Randomly builds foursomes as players sign up. Reshuffles when someone joins or bails. No politics. Just the draw.
⛅
Fore!cast
Weekend forecast auto-loads on the signup page. Conditions, high temp, and rain % for every active day. 16-day range covers both weekends.
📸
Pics — The 19th Hole Wall
Post course shots, celebrations, the drive that finally went straight. Photos live forever — never wiped week to week.
🏠
The Bunker
A second photo wall with a custom name for your group. For the bad lies, wrong clubs, and shots that should never be spoken of again.
🏆
Hole in One
Password-protected wall for those who've achieved the impossible. This wall means something.
🏌️
The Playa Club
All-time round tracker with gold, silver, bronze leaderboard. Only completed rounds count — sign up and bail and you get nothing.
⚙️
Smart Weekly Reset
Resets automatically every Monday. Clears last weekend, tallies Playa Club rounds, preserves advance signups. The app runs itself.
