What It Is

Sheet-Tee is a private, custom-built weekend coordination app for recreational golf groups. Instead of drowning in endless "who's in?" group texts every Friday night, your group gets their own private app — branded with your club name and logo — where members sign up for the weekend, watch foursomes build in real time, check the weather forecast, and track an all-time leaderboard.

No download required. Sheet-Tee runs entirely in your mobile browser. Your group gets one private URL they bookmark — or tap "Add to Home Screen" for an icon that opens full screen just like a native app. No app store, no account creation, no "how do I use this?" texts.

A group text is chaos. Sheet-Tee is structure. Instead of 40 messages every Friday night trying to figure out who's in, who's out, and what time — your group opens the app, taps their name, and the foursomes build automatically. No back-and-forth, no confusion, no "wait I thought you were playing."

Features

They tap their name to deselect — takes two seconds. Randos, our automatic foursome builder, reshuffles instantly. Everyone sees the updated groups in real time. No group text required.

Yes — Fore!cast is built right into the signup page. It shows the high temperature, conditions, and rain percentage for every active day, pre-set to your home course's exact GPS coordinates. The 16-day forecast covers both weekends so you always know what you're walking into.

The Playa Club is Sheet-Tee's all-time round tracker and leaderboard. Every completed round counts toward your ranking — Gold, Silver, and Bronze tiers. Sign up and bail? That round doesn't count. It's the one feature that gets guys showing up even when the weather is questionable.

The Bunker is a private photo wall for the shots that should never leave the group. Fat wedges, 4-putts, drives that went somewhere they shouldn't. Your group names it whatever they want — "The Divot Bar", "The Penalty Box", whatever fits your crew's sense of humor. It's separate from the main Pics wall and only accessible to your group.

The Immortal Realm. A password-protected photo wall exclusively for documented holes in one. If it's not on the wall, it didn't happen. This one means something.

Yes — the Guest Signup feature lets you add a visitor or sub to the roster for the weekend. They show up in the foursomes just like regular members. Guests don't carry over to the next week automatically.

The Comish handles everything automatically. Every Monday it tallies completed rounds into the Playa Club leaderboard, migrates any advance week-2 signups forward, clears the previous weekend's data, and resets for the new week. The app runs itself — no admin work needed.
